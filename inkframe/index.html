Your recipes, organized

Fujifilm photographers love using recipes: the Kodak Gold 200 afternoons, Reggie's Portra portraits, or Pacific Blues beach walks. InkFrame makes organization easy. Every shot tagged with its recipe, every EXIF field at a glance.

Built for in-camera enthusiasts

Not a filter. Not a preset. A recipe is a system — and InkFrame treats it like one.

  • Fingerprinted recipes
  • Group and filter photos by recipe
  • Import recipes by pasting a URL
  • Compare recipes with smart near-match detection
  • Sync from community sources
  • Sensor generation awareness

Every border tells the story

Export with Polaroid or Instagram 4:5 borders. Camera, lens, settings, film simulation name, and color palette swatches composited in.
